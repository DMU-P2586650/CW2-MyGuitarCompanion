The Head

The head of the guitar is where the tuning pegs and the nut is found. It is used to adjust the tune of the guitar and how far apart the strings are. This affects the tune and ring of the guitar.

The Neck

The neck of the guitar is where the magic happens. Home to the fret board (the white line), the neck of the guitar is where different chords snd notes are created. With basic tuning, each string represents a different note: EADGBe. To easily remember this use the phrase "Eddie Ate Dynamite Good Bye eddie"

The Body

The body of the guitar is where the sound is created. On an accoustic guitar, the hole under teh strings is called the sound hole which is where the sound comes from, hence the name. By strumming the strings, vibrations resonate in the body of the guitar and projected through the sound hole.
